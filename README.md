# Cyber-Threat-Intelligence-CTI-
In this lab, you will take on the role of a Junior Cybersecurity Analyst at SecureHealth, a mid-sized healthcare organization specializing in providing electronic medical record (EMR) software and telehealth services. Recently, SecureHealth has experienced a rise in ransomware attacks targeting its systems, resulting in disruptions to patient care and concerns about data privacy. In response, SecureHealth is implementing a Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) strategy to improve its proactive defense measures and response to threats.

Organization’s Threat Intelligence Objectives
    . Enhance Ransomware Detection and Mitigation: Improve the ability to detect and contain ransomware attacks before         they spread across the network.
    . Protect Patient Data and Ensure Compliance: Implement measures to secure EMRs and maintain compliance with HIPAA         and other healthcare regulations.
    . Improve Incident Response Capabilities: Strengthen response strategies to ensure continuity of operations during a       ransomware attack.
Technology Environment
    . Threat Intelligence Platform: SecureHealth uses Recorded Future to integrate threat intelligence feeds and               facilitate proactive analysis.
    . Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR): CrowdStrike is used for endpoint security and incident response.
    . Data Backup and Recovery: SecureHealth employs Veeam for frequent backups and recovery to minimize downtime during       attacks.
Risk Assessment Summary
    . Ransomware Attacks: High likelihood and high impact, given the critical nature of patient care systems.
    . Unauthorized Access: Stolen credentials leading to system breaches.
    . Third-Party Risk: Vendors with access to sensitive systems are a weak link.
Stakeholder Requirements
    . IT Security Team: Requires intelligence on ransomware tactics and Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) to improve EDR         configurations.
    . Compliance Team: Needs detailed intelligence to ensure adherence to HIPAA regulations.
    . Incident Response Team: Seeks actionable intelligence to speed containment and recovery.

# Instructions
Using the provided information from the scenario and the template, complete the following areas of the template.
1. Define Objectives and Scope
2. Conduct a Threat Intelligence Needs Assessment
3. Gather and Integrate Intelligence Sources
4. Develop Threat Intelligence Processes
5. Build CTI Capabilities and Infrastructure
